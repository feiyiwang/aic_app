Introduction


We are excited to introduce aiC.health, a clinical decision support software that streamlines the process of navigating through medical history data. Using machine learning and natural language processing, we provide a tool that collects all available data from across the healthcare provider's system, highlighting relevant information to help them get familiar with the patient's medical history at-a-glance. A sophisticated and powerful timeline visualisation enables doctors and nurses to find the relevant information for each visit and eliminates excessive time waste. With aiC.health, medical professionals can make better-informed decisions, improve patient outcomes, and increase efficiency in their work. As the market of clinical decision support systems is rapidly growing (reaching 12.4 B USD by 2030), we are expecting a high impact on the industry with our SaaS business.

Join our journey in providing more efficient healthcare for everyone.

Features:

Extract clinical events from notes with date and show these notes on a timeline
